The Best Movies According to Ti-a

My top 3 Movies of all-time.

Love & Basketball

"Love & Basketball" is one of those movies that gets me every single time. The ups and downs of the characters' relationship, their love for basketball, and how they always find their way back to each other is just amazing. Seriously, if you haven't seen it yet, you need to watch it! This film is a huge part of my childhood. I love how it shows romance and sports from a woman’s perspective. It’s so inspiring to see how she pours her heart into both her relationship and basketball, a sport that’s usually seen as a guy thing. It’s a great reminder for women to chase their dreams no matter what people think. Even though it's over twenty years old, I still love watching it. The story of young love, all the challenges they face, and figuring out their careers is so real and relatable. Plus, the actors were just starting out, and they did an awesome job. This is one of those classics everyone should see at least once.

The Fall Guy

"The Fall Guy" is an absolute blast from start to finish! This movie had me hooked with its thrilling stunts, humor, and action-packed scenes. If you’re looking for something fun and entertaining, this is definitely a must-watch. I love the fact that "The Fall Guy" pays tribute to the unsung heroes of stunt doubles. It gives you a behind-the-scenes look at the risky world they navigate, which adds a whole new layer of appreciation to the movie.

The Upside

Based on a true story, it's a beautiful tale of Dell, a parolee hired as caregiver to a billionaire quadriplegic named Philip. Their journey teaches profound life lessons about acceptance and friendship. Kevin Hart and Bryan Cranston deliver superb performances, blending comedy and heartwarming moments seamlessly. The film's highlights include joyrides, a hilarious blind date, and a touching finale with paragliding and opera music. Absolutely loved it and highly recommend!
